Hello! My name is Blake Morgan. I am a high jumper at Northwest Missouri State University.

My Background

I grew up in a small town called Kirksville, Missouri. I have played almost every sport imaginable, but chose to play football, basketball, and run track in high school. I graduated from Kirksville High School in the spring of 2019 and am now part of the Class of 2023 at Northwest Missouri State University.
